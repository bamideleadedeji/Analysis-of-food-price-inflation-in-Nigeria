Data is provided by the National Bureau of Statistics, Nigeria.(NBS).
The data reveals a story of persistent and broad-based food inflation, 
with animal proteins (beef, fish, chicken) being the primary drivers. 
Regional analysis shows the South East and South South geopolitical zones 
bearing the highest costs, while the North East often has the lowest. 
Staples like rice and beans have seen moderate increases,
but condiments like vegetable oil have experienced shocking price surges
